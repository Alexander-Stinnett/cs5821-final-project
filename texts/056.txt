Houghton, Michigan is a charming college town on the Keweenaw Peninsula that offers excellent outdoor recreation year-round. In winter, you can enjoy world-class snowmobiling, cross-country skiing, and downhill skiing at nearby Mont Ripley or Mount Bohemia. During warmer months, explore the waterfront along Portage Lake, hike scenic trails like the Nara Nature Park, or visit the historic downtown with its local shops and breweries. The area is also perfect for mountain biking, kayaking, and experiencing the stunning fall colors that blanket the surrounding forests.
